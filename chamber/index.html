Current Events

Vigan City recently hosted a vibrant festival that showcased the rich tapestry of its local culture. The festivities highlighted traditional crafts such as weaving and clay pot making, providing a platform for artisans to display their intricate skills. The city's streets came alive with colorful parades and cultural performances, embodying the spirit of unity and heritage. Visitors immersed themselves in the artistry of handwoven textiles and intricately crafted clay pots, celebrating the city's dedication to preserving these time-honored traditions.

Tourism Information

Vigan City, a UNESCO World Heritage site, beckons with its timeless charm. Immerse yourself in centuries-old cobblestone streets and Spanish colonial architecture. Indulge in local delicacies and embrace the unique blend of history and culture that makes Vigan a must-visit destination.
