Membership Benefits

NP Membership (Free)

  • Access to Chamber networking events.
  • Listing in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Participation in Chamber committees and task forces.
  • Subscription to Chamber newsletters and updates.

Bronze Membership ($50)

  • All benefits of NP Membership.
  • Discounted rates for Chamber-sponsored workshops and seminars.
  • Recognition as a Bronze Member in Chamber publications and events.
  • Priority access to Chamber resources and services.

Silver Membership ($100)

  • All benefits of Bronze Membership.
  • Invitations to exclusive Chamber luncheons with industry leaders.
  • Enhanced visibility through sponsorship opportunities at Chamber events.
  • Access to market research and business development resources.

Gold Membership ($200)

  • All benefits of Silver Membership.
  • Featured spotlight on the Chamber's website and social media platforms.
  • Complimentary passes to premium Chamber events and conferences.
  • Dedicated support from Chamber staff for business inquiries and advocacy efforts.
